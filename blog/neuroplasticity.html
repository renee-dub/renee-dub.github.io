Neuroplasticity

What are the benefits of neuroplasticity?

When people are aware that their brain is in fact malleable and you can work to both maintain and create neural pathways, trying something new whether a hobby or study, seems less daunting and more achievable. Your ability to learn new things increases, you can solidify what you already know, it allows you to be resilient and recover from setbacks more easily and your passion for learning new things increases.

How can you engage with these benefits?

For me, I have found that my curiosity wins each time I am stuck or struggling with a new concept, when I remember that I can change the way I think and build new connections in my brain by practicing, practicing, practicing and visualising myself doing the task. I like to visualise the neurons firing to make new pathways and connections in my brain and try to have an open mind to what I am learning and remind myself that this is a new subject and it will take time to change my brainâ€¦ for the better.

What is growth mindset?

Growth mindset is the belief that you CAN learn something new and that you might not be an expert on something YET, but you will be if you know that your brain is malleable and you are capable of changing the way you think and learn. When you blur the line between hard success and failure, you can see that if you put in the time and effort and are supported along the way, you can learn something new and grow your mindset.
