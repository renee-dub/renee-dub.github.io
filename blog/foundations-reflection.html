Core Reflections

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

  • That I can learn something new, even if I have pre-conceived ideas about what is required to do well in a tech role.
  • That I approach new learnings with curiosity but also resistance due to my lack of self-confidence. I just need to work on letting curiosity win!
  • That I keep trying, no matter how long or how many shots it takes to get something right.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Being true to yourself while also being empathetic and understanding of others is a huge part of navigating the real world. Being able to collaborate and communicate with others is a skill that can be overlooked in more technical roles but it is so important to ensure yourself and your peers are happy and able to positively and constructively work through any challenging situations. Being aware of yourself, how you react to certain situations or where your strengths and skills lie are great tools to help guide you to what you need to focus on while learning, how you learn and how to ask for help in a way that is conducive to your learning style.
