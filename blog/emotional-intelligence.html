Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to identify and manage your own emotions, and the emotions of others.

Five Elements of Emotional Intelligence.

  • Self-Awareness
  • Self-Regulation
  • Motivation
  • Empathy
  • Social Skills

How is Emotional Intelligence Different to Intellectual Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to recognise and manage your own, and others, emotions, and change them accordingly, while Intellectual intelligence is your ability to learn and problem solve using logic and reason.

Why is Emotional Intelligence Important?

Emotional intelligence is important as being able to understand where someone is coming from, why they are feeling a particular emotion, and the ability to build rapport with them makes working together much easier. People feel more at ease in group situations and more comfortable being themselves and asking questions when they feel seen and heard and understood.
