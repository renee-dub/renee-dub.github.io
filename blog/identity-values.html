Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey. Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?

Strengths: curious, perfectionist, detail-orientated.
My curious and detail-orientated nature will help me to deep dive into the curriculum and learn all I can. I want to know how things work, I want to know why, and these traits definitely help with that! These will help me to learn new concepts and speed up my learning process and introduce me to things that I haven't even heard of yet, and potentially gain a new skillset that will help me to secure a job once the course has finished while still continuing to learn.

Limitations: procrastination, lack of self-confidence, not asking for help when I really should.
I have already noticed that putting tasks off, or skipping ahead if a particular task seems to daunting or not asking for help has negative effects on my learning. It becomes harder to go back and attempt a task and then circle back to where you're supposed to be which is very confusing and doesn't help with procrastinating or feeling confident in my abilities. I want to be able to confront my fear of doing something wrong or not knowing something head-on and jump straight in and be curious.
